Tap a tile → panel expands below with a scrollable list of options. Sorted by performance. Pick one, panel collapses, tile shows your selection. Everything stays on one page.

C: AI Picks, You Override

AI pre-selects the best combo based on performance data. User sees the suggestion and can either accept ("Generate with this") or customize any tile. Power users override; casual users just click Generate. Lowest friction.

Note: These aren't mutually exclusive. C (AI picks) could use A (inline) or B (drawer) when you tap to customize. The question is: what's the default experience?
